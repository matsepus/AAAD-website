Hail To The Thief
Radiohead
This album is not one of my favorites from Radiohead, but if you try not to compare it to their other masterpieces you realise that is still way a grat work of art. It has some really good songs like 2+2=5 and There There, but also some fillers like Sail to the Moon. Overall I give it a solid 3 out of 5 stars.
